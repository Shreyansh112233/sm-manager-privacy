Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2026

SM Manager respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your data associated with this app, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data in one of the following ways:

  • Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject line:
    “Data Deletion Request”
  • Include the email address used to log in to the app (Facebook or Email).

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verification, we will delete:

  • Your authentication account
  • Any stored profile information
  • Associated app usage data

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 7 business days.
